About Me - The NYC Edition 🗽

Hi! I'm Etasha (but you can just call me Etash wink wink). I'm a student in Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo, which, by the way, is the 11th largest country in the world. We have more resources than a treasure chest, but I’m more into quiet mornings, sipping tea, reading books, and jamming out to my favorite playlists. I’m a writer, coder, and book lover. Welcome to my personal website — where sophistication meets humor. 🎉
